Recovery Guide

Immediate Burn First Aid

A burn injury can be scary in the moment. This quick guide summarizes the first steps families can take while seeking appropriate medical care.

Download / Print This Guide

Quick steps

Three first steps families can remember

1

Cool the burn

Run cool, not cold, water over the burn. Avoid ice, butter, oil, toothpaste, or popping blisters.

2

Remove clothing or jewelry near the burn

Gently remove nearby clothing, diapers, rings, bracelets, or other items unless they are stuck to the skin.

3

Cover with a clean cloth or dressing

Use a clean cloth or dressing to protect the area while arranging the right next step for care.
