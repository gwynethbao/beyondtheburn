Pediatric burn recovery support

Helping children and families navigate life after a burn injury.

Beyond the Burn brings together survivor stories, practical recovery resources, and trusted guidance for families navigating the months and years after a child’s burn injury.

Read Stories Explore Recovery Guide

Why this exists

Recovery does not end when the wound closes.

Burn recovery can involve scar care, follow-up appointments, painful treatments, returning to school, emotional adjustment, and questions families may not know how to ask. This project was created to help families feel more informed, supported, and less alone.
